SustainaBowl

A full-cycle Android application that tracks available ingredients and generates personalized recipes based on what is on hand.

What the project shows

SustainaBowl shows that I can move through the full product cycle, from research and prototypes to testing and deployment.

It is the strongest example of how I combine UX thinking with development work because the final app started from user needs, not just a technical idea.

Why it belongs here

This project gives employers a direct look at the kind of work I want to keep doing: useful, user-centered, and built with a clear purpose.
